    Our company uses high quality PVC material, fabric, aluminum, mirror, metal ceilings, which meets all quality standards, blaze safety and sanitation adopted in the world. The company was founded in 2001 year. Since its inception and until today our main specialization is production of stretch ceilings, wholesale and retail trade of tension ceilings and the accompanying goods.

  • Наша история.

    2001-2004

    Opened its first sales office. The company operates as a dealer sells and installs ceilings and partitions from another manufacturer.

    2005

    Built its own factory. Park equipment updated: new automated machine tools increasingly replace manual labor and prevent possible errors-the so-called "human factor" in production.

    2008-2009

    The production capacity is increased and modernized. Built a new plant with an area of one thousand square meters. The total area of the plant has reached two thousand square meters. On the establishment of urban new buildings work till 40 installation teams simultaneously.

    2013

    The production capacity of the plant capable of producing is to 300 square meters of plastic designs and up to 100 square metres of aluminium. We use only components of known and proven suppliers.
